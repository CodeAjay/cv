Over the course of 20 years he has worked in the role of Scientist, Professor, Executive Director, Entrepreneur and Consultant across several industries including Electrical Engineering, Education, Human Development, and Financial Services. This diverse experience, coupled with a global perspective and a passion for learning, has given him a thorough understanding of the strategic & tactical issues as well as the human capital needed for sustainable value creation. His areas of expertise are high performance teams, transformatve leadership and innovation capacity development.

Dr. Hijazi lived in five countries and speaks Arabic, English, Spanish and Italian fluently.
